Privacy Policy for Modern Productivity Hub

Last Updated: May 26, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Modern Productivity Hub extension ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • User Preferences: Such as your display name, color theme preferences, and other customization settings
  • Browser Bookmarks: To display your bookmarks within the extension interface
  • Browsing History: To provide website analytics features that help you track your productivity

Important: All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Customize your new tab experience according to your preferences
  • Display your bookmarks for easy access
  • Provide website analytics to help you track your productivity
  • Remember your settings between browser sessions

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage mechanisms (localStorage and chrome.storage API). Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

The extension may connect to the following third-party services:

  • OpenWeatherMap API: If you choose to use the weather widget and provide an API key, the extension will make requests to OpenWeatherMap to fetch weather data. These requests include only your location information (city name or coordinates) as needed to provide weather data.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Clear all stored data by clearing your browser's local storage
  • Uninstall the extension at any time to remove all associated data
  • Modify your settings within the extension's settings panel

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
